GymPrtnr

is a web-based application based on the idea of bringing people together to enjoy physical activities. After having spent many years going to the gym without a partner, I thought it would be nice to have means of meeting other folks that participate in the same activities as I do.

GymPrtnr

utilizes Rails as a backend, Angular.js and JQuery on the front-end, PostGres as a database. This application was built as the third and final project during my coursework at General Assembly
